## 3. Pitch Frameworks

### The App Launch Press Release

**Headline formula:** "[Brand] Launches [App] Giving [Audience] [Benefit] for the First Time"

**Example:**
> "My Keeper Coach Launches App That Brings Professional Goalkeeper Development Tracking to Youth Soccer Coaches Nationwide"

**Press release structure:**
1. **Lead (2–3 sentences):** The news, the significance, the brand
2. **Quote (founder):** Vision, why this matters, who it's for
3. **Problem context:** The gap in the market this fills
4. **Key features:** 3 bullets, no jargon
5. **User quote:** Real coach describing the impact
6. **Availability:** Free on iOS, link to App Store
7. **Boilerplate:** Company description, contact

### The Expert Pitch (Thought Leadership)

**What to offer:** Your commentary as a goalkeeper development expert on a trending topic

**Pitch timing triggers:**
- Youth soccer season starts → "3 things coaches need to know about developing goalkeepers this season"
- Youth sports safety news → "How structured training prevents goalkeeper injuries"
- Youth sports burnout trend → "Age-appropriate development as the antidote to youth soccer burnout"
- US Soccer player development updates → "What this means for youth goalkeeper coaching"

**Expert pitch email template:**
```
Subject: Youth goalkeeper development expert available for [Topic] story

Hi [Journalist first name],

I saw your piece on [specific article] and wanted to reach out. 

I'm the founder of My Keeper Coach, an app used by [X] youth soccer coaches to track goalkeeper development. I've spent [X] years working on youth goalkeeper development and have a few data points / perspectives on [Topic] that your readers might find genuinely useful.

Happy to offer 15 minutes on the phone or a few quotes by email — whatever works for your timeline.

Best,
[Name]
```

### The Human Interest Pitch

The most powerful PR for My Keeper Coach will come from real keeper stories.

**Story structure to find and pitch:**
1. The keeper who was struggling / losing confidence
2. The coach who started using structured tracking
3. The first breakthrough moment (first badge, first clean sheet, first progress report)
4. The transformation: keeper's confidence today

**Pitch to:** Local newspapers, youth soccer publications, parenting blogs, soccer parent social media communities

## 5. Product Hunt Launch Strategy

### Product Hunt is a Major PR Event

A well-executed Product Hunt launch can drive thousands of installs in a single day and generate tech press coverage.

**Pre-Launch (2 weeks before):**
- Build a "hunter" relationship with an established Product Hunt hunter (5K+ followers)
- Prepare assets: GIF demo, screenshots, description, video
- Build a waitlist of supporters who will upvote on launch day

**Launch Day:**
- Post at 12:01 AM PST (when Product Hunt day resets)
- Coordinate 50+ upvotes in the first 2 hours (critical for homepage position)
- Respond to every comment personally throughout the day
- Share to every channel simultaneously: Twitter/X, LinkedIn, email list, Slack communities

**Post-Launch:**
- Badge ("Featured on Product Hunt") on website forever
- Pitch journalists: "We launched on Product Hunt and [X] coaches signed up in [timeframe]"
