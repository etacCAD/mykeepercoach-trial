## 2. Content Framework

### The TikTok Content Hierarchy (Priority Order)

```
1. NATIVE CONTENT (filmed on iPhone, authentic, no heavy editing)
2. EDUCATIONAL HOOKS ("Things your goalkeeper coach never told you")
3. POV / RELATABLE ("POV: Your U12 keeper finally claims their first cross")
4. TRANSFORMATION ("4 weeks of tracking vs. coaching blind")
5. APP DEMO (genuinely interesting feature reveals, not ads)
```

### Content Categories

**Category 1: "Things Most GK Coaches Get Wrong" (Authority + Virality)**
- Format: Talking head + text overlay + B-roll of goalkeeper actions
- Hook structure: Lead with the controversial statement
- Examples:
  - "You should NOT be teaching your U9 goalkeeper to dive yet — here's why 🧵"
  - "Stop asking your keeper their save %. This is what you should ask instead."
  - "The #1 mistake I see in youth goalkeeper coaching (and it's destroying confidence)"

**Category 2: "Age Group Deep Dives" (Educational, Searchable)**
- Format: Quick educational cuts, split screen, graphic overlays
- Examples:
  - "What a U10 goalkeeper should be working on (most coaches skip this)"
  - "U13 keepers — this is the year everything changes. Here's what to focus on."
  - "The vault skill every U16 keeper must master before college showcases"

**Category 3: POV Series (Relatability + Shares)**
- Format: Text-overlay POV scenarios; no voiceover needed
- Examples:
  - "POV: Your quietest keeper earns their first clean sheet badge 🔒"
  - "POV: You're a parent watching your kid's first match report card generate"
  - "POV: Your U14 keeper finally figures out angle play 📊"

**Category 4: App Features (Disguised as Content)**
- Never lead with "download our app" — lead with the problem the feature solves
- Format: Screen recording + voiceover showing a real use case
- Examples:
  - "How I track my keeper's development without a spreadsheet or notebook"
  - "What I see as a coach after every training session [app demo]"
  - "The milestone system that keeps my young keepers motivated all season"

**Category 5: Trending Audio Adaptation**
- Monitor TikTok trending sounds weekly
- Adapt goalkeeper/coaching content to fit trending formats
- Quick turnaround is critical — trend windows are 48–72 hours

## 3. Hook Mastery (First 2 Seconds = Everything)

### Hook Formulas That Convert

| Hook Type | Template | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Controversy** | "Stop doing [common thing]" | "Stop teaching U10 goalkeepers to dive" |
| **Curiosity gap** | "The [secret/rule/thing] about [topic] that nobody talks about" | "The rule about youth GK development nobody teaches" |
| **Transformation** | "[Before] → [After] in [timeframe]" | "This keeper went from quitting to confident in 4 sessions" |
| **List** | "[Number] things [group] should know" | "3 things every goalkeeper parent needs to hear" |
| **POV** | "POV: [relatable scenario]" | "POV: Your keeper earns their first Iron Gloves badge 🧤" |
| **Question** | "Why do [X] when you could [Y]?" | "Why track saves when you should be tracking decisions?" |

### Hook Optimization Protocol

1. **Write 5 hooks per video** before filming — a mediocre video with a great hook outperforms a great video with a mediocre hook
2. **Test hooks via Stories** on Instagram before committing to a TikTok
3. **First frame matters** — freeze frame must be visually compelling with or without sound

## 4. Algorithm Intelligence

### TikTok Ranking Signals (2025–2026)

| Signal | Weight | How to Optimize |
|---|---|---|
| **Completion Rate** | Highest | Keep videos short (15–30s initially), hook → value → CTA |
| **Rewatch Rate** | Very High | Include "pause-worthy" moments, dense information |
| **Shares** | High | Create content people send to their friends/coaches |
| **Comments** | Medium-High | Ask a question at the end of every video |
| **Saves** | Medium | Make content worth referring back to |
| **Likes** | Lower | Good but not primary signal |

### Posting Cadence

- **Minimum:** 1 video per day (TikTok rewards consistency heavily)
- **Optimal:** 2–3 videos per day during growth phase
- **Reply with video:** Always respond to interesting comments with a video reply — massive engagement signal

### Best Posting Times (Eastern)

| Day | Best Windows |
|---|---|
| Monday–Friday | 7–9 AM, 12–2 PM, 7–9 PM |
| Saturday | 9 AM–12 PM |
| Sunday | 2–5 PM |

## 5. TikTok-Specific Tactics

### Duet & Stitch Strategy

- **Stitch youth soccer coaching content** — add your goalkeeper-specific take
- **Duet viral goalkeeper saves** — provide coaching analysis overlay
- Creates discovery through the original video's audience

### Series Strategy (Algorithm Loves Series)

Create ongoing series with consistent naming for discoverability:

| Series | Format | CTA |
|---|---|---|
| "GK Coach Tips #[N]" | One coaching tip per episode | "Follow for GK Tip #[N+1]" |
| "Age Group Breakdown" | U8/U11/U14/U17 spotlights | "Which age group next? Comment below" |
| "App Update Breakdown" | New feature reveals | "Comment if you want this feature" |

### Comment Strategy

- Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours of posting (critical for early algorithm boost)
- Pin a comment that adds context or encourages conversation
- Use comments to seed the next video: "Next video, I'll cover [X] — stay tuned"
