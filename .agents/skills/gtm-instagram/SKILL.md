## 2. Content Strategy

### Reels (Primary Growth Driver)

**Format: Educational Coaching Tips**
- Duration: 15–30 seconds
- Hook (first 1.5 seconds): Bold text overlay of the tip + compelling visual
- Example hooks that work:
  - *"STOP teaching your U10 keeper to dive"*
  - *"This 30-second drill fixes 90% of positioning errors"*
  - *"Most youth coaches don't know the difference between these 2 saves"*

**High-performing Reel series:**

| Series | Concept | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| 🧤 "Keeper Tip of the Week" | One specific coaching tip per skill pillar | Weekly |
| 📊 "Did You Know?" | Stats/facts about goalkeeper development | 2x/month |
| 🎬 "Before & After" | Keeper technique transformation over 4 weeks | Monthly |
| 🚫 "Stop Doing This" | Common coaching mistakes at each age group | 2x/month |
| ⚽ "Age Group Spotlight" | Deep dive into U8–U10 / U11–U13 / U14–U16 development | Monthly |

**Reel Production Rules:**
- Always start with a visual hook — never a talking head for the first 2 seconds
- Add captions — 85% of Reels watched without sound
- Use trending audio when relevant, but never sacrifice message clarity
- End with ONE clear CTA: "Follow for more" OR "Link in bio to download"
- Never two CTAs in the same video

### Carousels (Saves & Shares King)

Best for: Step-by-step guides, comparison breakdowns, frameworks

**Top-performing carousel formats:**
1. **"The X-Step Guide to [Skill]"** — "The 5-Step Guide to Teaching the Collapse Dive"
2. **"U[X] vs U[Y]"** — "What U8 Keepers Need vs. What U13 Keepers Need"
3. **"[Common Mistake] and How to Fix It"** — swipe-through with visual diagrams
4. **Age-gated development breakdown** — the full stage progression from U8–U18

**Carousel rules:**
- Slide 1 = hook (make people swipe)
- Final slide = always a CTA + save prompt
- Keep to 5–8 slides max
- Use the My Keeper Coach design system colors for brand consistency

### Stories (Daily Touchpoint)

| Story Type | Purpose | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Coaching tips (text/graphic) | Education, link traffic | Daily |
| Polls ("agree or disagree?") | Engagement signal | 3x/week |
| Q&A stickers ("Ask me anything about GK training") | Community, content ideas | Weekly |
| Behind the scenes | Authenticity | 2x/week |
| App features demo | Conversion | 2x/week |
| User-generated content (reposts) | Social proof | When available |

## 6. Platform Algorithm Intelligence

### Current Instagram Algorithm Priorities (2025–2026)

1. **Reels > everything** — the algorithm pushes Reels to non-followers
2. **Send rate** (how many times people share to DMs) — highest-value signal
3. **Saves** — second-highest signal; all educational content should be save-worthy
4. **Comments** — reply to every comment within 1 hour of posting
5. **Watch-through rate** — longer watch = more distribution; front-load value
