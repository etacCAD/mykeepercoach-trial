## 2. Blog / SEO Content

### Headline Formulas That Work for Coaches

- **"How to [specific outcome] for [specific age group]"**
  → "How to Coach a U10 Goalkeeper Through Their First Season"
  
- **"The [Number] [Thing] Every [Audience] Should Know About [Topic]"**
  → "The 8 Goalkeeper Skills Every U13 Coach Should Be Developing Right Now"

- **"Why [Common Practice] Is Wrong (And What to Do Instead)"**
  → "Why Shooting Drills at the End of Practice Aren't Developing Your Keeper"

- **"[Age Group] Goalkeeper Development: The Complete Guide"**
  → "U11-U13 Goalkeeper Development: The Complete Training Guide"

### Blog Post Structure (1,200–2,000 words)

```
1. Hook (150 words) — Open with the specific pain or scenario
2. The Problem (200 words) — Name what's happening and why it matters
3. The Framework / Answer (600–900 words) — The actual valuable content, broken into 3-5 sections with H2s
4. What App Solves (200 words) — Natural, non-salesy product mention
5. CTA (100 words) — One action: "Start your free trial"
```

**SEO requirements (coordinate with SEO Expert):**
- Primary keyword in H1 and first 100 words
- H2s use keyword variations
- Internal link to at least 2 other blog posts
- Include a "Key Takeaways" section for featured snippet targeting

## 3. Social Captions

### Instagram Caption Structure

**Formula: Hook → Context → Value → Soft CTA**

```
[Hook — 1 line, forces a pause]

[Context — 1-2 sentences, why this matters]

[Value — 3-5 bullet points or a short story]

[Soft CTA — not aggressive, conversation-starting]

[Hashtags — 8-12, mix of niche + broad]
```

**Example — Education pillar:**
```
Most youth goalkeepers are being coached for the wrong age.

U10 keepers shouldn't be worrying about distribution. Their joints are still developing. Pushing advanced technique too early builds bad habits — and erodes confidence.

Here's what U10 keepers actually need:
• Reaction and agility over diving technique
• Fun-first training environments
• Positive reinforcement on effort, not outcomes
• Basic handling confidence before anything else

What age group are you working with right now? Drop it below 👇

#youthgoalkeeper #goalkeepingcoach #gkcoach #youthsoccer #goalkeeperdevelopment #soccercoach #keepercoach #u10soccer #grassrootscoaching #goalkeepingtraining
```

### TikTok Script Structure

```
[0–2 sec: HOOK — visual + spoken. Pattern interrupt.]
[2–8 sec: SETUP — what's the problem or thing they're about to learn]
[8–35 sec: PAYLOAD — the actual value. Fast. Specific. No padding.]
[35–45 sec: CTA + LOOP — "Follow for more" or question that triggers comments]
```

**Hook bank (goalkeeper-specific):**
- "Your goalkeeper is making one mistake that costs them every match..."
- "I analyzed 50 match videos and found something every youth keeper does wrong"
- "The one drill that transformed this U13 goalkeeper in 4 weeks"
- "Why 90% of goalkeeping coaches focus on the wrong thing"
- "Here's what a professional report card looks like for a 12-year-old keeper"

## 5. Email Copy Principles

*(Coordinate with Email & Lifecycle Expert for sequence strategy)*

**Subject line rules:**
- Under 50 characters when possible (mobile-first)
- Specific > clever: "Your report card is ready" beats "Big news inside"
- Test 2 variants for every send with > 500-person lists

**Email body rules:**
- First sentence must be a continuation of the subject line — no re-introduction
- Short paragraphs: 2-3 sentences max
- One CTA per email. One. Not two.
- End every non-transactional email with a question that invites a reply

## 6. Content Calendar Management

### Weekly Content Output Targets

| Channel | Output | Pillar Mix |
|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 3 videos/week | 2 Education, 1 Proof |
| Instagram | 4 posts/week | 2 Education, 1 Proof, 1 Community |
| Blog | 2 posts/week | SEO-driven keyword topics |
| Email newsletter | 1/month | All pillars |

### Monthly Content Sprint Process

1. **Week 4 of previous month** — CMO sets content themes for next month
2. **Day 1** — Content Strategist maps all content slots to pillars and keywords
3. **Day 2-3** — Write all long-form content first (blog, email)
4. **Day 4-5** — Repurpose long-form into social content
5. **Day 6** — Review + schedule all content
6. **Day 7+** — Monitor performance, optimize underperformers

### Content Repurposing Hierarchy

```
1 Blog Post (2,000 words)
    → 1 Instagram carousel (10 slides)
    → 2 TikTok scripts (hook from blog intro)
    → 3 Instagram captions (key insights)
    → 1 Email section (for monthly newsletter)
    → 5 Twitter/X one-liners
```
