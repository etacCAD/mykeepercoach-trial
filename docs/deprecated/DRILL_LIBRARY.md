# Goalkeeper Drill Library
### 3 Key Drills × Skill Pillar × Age Stage

> Each skill pillar has 3 drills per age group. One drill in every set is marked **🏠 AT HOME** — something the keeper can do solo with minimal equipment (a ball, a wall, and a small space).
>
> Drills are only listed for age stages where the skill pillar is developmentally appropriate, matching the [Goalie Development Framework](file:///Users/evanhopkins/Projects/play%20AT/Goalie%20Coach/GOALIE_DEVELOPMENT_FRAMEWORK.md).

# 1. SHOT STOPPING

## U8–U10 — Foundation

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wall Ball Catch** | 🏠 | Ball, wall | Stand 3–5 ft from wall. Throw against wall at varying heights. Catch with W or Basket hands. | Hands early to ball, eyes track ball into gloves, move feet to get body behind. | 3 sets of 20 catches. | **Harder:** Use a tennis ball or decrease distance to wall. |
| 2 | **Coach Roll & Catch** | 👥 | Ball, cones | Coach rolls ball firmly to left, right, and center from 6 yards. Keeper gets set, moves, scoops. | Ready position, shuffle to get behind ball, secure scoop technique. | 10 reps each side (left/center/right). | **Harder:** Coach increases speed of roll or adds a second ball. |
| 3 | **Rainbow Catching** | 👥 | Ball | Coach lobs ball gently (underhand) to different heights — waist, chest, head height. | Correct hand shape for height (W vs Basket), stable base, high catch point. | 15 lobs per set. | **Harder:** Keeper starts with back to coach, turns on command "Ball!" and reacts. |

## U11–U13 — Skill Acquisition

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wall Rapid Fire** | 🏠 | Ball, wall | Stand 4–6 ft from wall. Throw hard, catch, touch ground, repeat. | Quick hands, soft catch, rapid recovery to set position. | 3 sets of 45 seconds. | **Harder:** Stand on one leg or use a smaller ball (e.g. tennis ball). |
| 2 | **Collapse Dive Progression** | 👥 | Ball, mat optional | **Phase 1-3:** Kneeling, standing low, shuffle + low save. | Sequence: thigh → hip → torso. Bottom hand behind ball. | 8 reps each side per phase. | **Harder:** Add a second ball for a 'double save' scenario. |
| 3 | **Angle Closing & Save** | 👥 | Ball, cones (goal), 3 spots | Set up 3 cones in triangle. Adjust angle for spot called, then save. | Repositioning footwork, getting "Set" (feet still & balanced) before shot. **Recover to Center**: Get back to the middle of the goal between shots. | 12 total reps (4 per spot). | **Harder:** Strikers can pass between each other before the shot. |

## U14–U16 — Skill Refinement

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Reaction Ball Wall Drill** | 🏠 | Reaction ball, wall ⚽ | Stand 6–8 ft from wall. Throw reaction ball — react and catch/parry. |peripheral vision, stay balanced, hand speed, react to bounce. | 4 sets of 45 seconds. | **Harder:** Step closer to the wall (faster reaction needed). |
| 2 | **Extension Dive Series** | 👥 | Ball, full goal | Coach serves from 12 yards to corners — make full extension dives. | Power step explosiveness, fingertip contact on far saves, safe landing. | 4 sets of 6 saves (alt sides). | **Harder:** Add a low hurdle to jump over before the dive. |
| 3 | **Deflection & Second Ball** | 👥 | 2 balls, goal, 2 servers | Server 1 shoots — save/parry. Server 2 immediately shoots rebound. | Parry direction (away from goal). **Recover to Center**: Quickly find the middle of the goal before shot 2. | 10 rounds (20 shots total). | **Harder:** Server 2 moves to change the angle before their second shot. |

## U17–U18 — Performance

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Shadow Diving Visualization** | 🏠 | Soft surface | Start in ready pos. Visualize a shot — execute a full dive. Reset. | Explosive first step, dive shape, smooth landing, recovery speed. | 5 sets of 6 dives. | **Harder:** Film yourself and review technique between sets. |
| 2 | **Game-Speed Shot Circuit** | 👥 | Balls, goal, 3 shooters ⚽ | Shooters at edges of 18 and penalty spot. Face rapid shots. | Repositioning speed, reading shooters. **Recover to Center**: Find the middle of the goal between shooters. | 4 rounds of 8 shots. | **Harder:** Shooters can pass between each other before the shot. |
| 3 | **xG Save Challenge** | 👥 | Balls, goal, data sheet ⚽ | Coach assigns xG values to shots. Target: Beat the xG score. | Statistical performance, mental composure, high-xG saves. | 2 sets of 20 shots. | **Harder:** Use a rebound board for unpredictable deflections. |

# 2. CROSSES & HIGH BALLS

> *Not assessed until U11. Active training begins at U13.*

## U11–U13 — Introduction

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wall Toss & Catch High** | 🏠 | Ball, high wall | Throw ball high against wall. Jump, catch at peak, land secure. | Timing of jump, secure W-hands at peak, land on both feet. | 3 sets of 15 catches. | **Harder:** Add a 1-step approach jump before catching. |
| 2 | **Simple Cross Claiming** | 👥 | Ball, goal, 1 server | Server delivers looping crosses. Keeper starts on line, comes to catch. | Decision to come, footwork to meeting point, claiming at max height. | 12 reps (6 per side). | **Harder:** Server adds more pace or lower trajectory to the cross. |
| 3 | **Coach Lob Challenge** | 👥 | Ball | Coach tosses from various angles. Keeper adjusts feet and catches. | Getting feet set under the ball, secure handling, body balance. | 15 reps. | **Harder:** Keeper starts facing the goal, turns on "Keeper!" call. |

## U14–U16 — Full Development

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Overhead Throw & Catch Solo** | 🏠 | Ball, open space | Throw ball as high as possible. Move under, time jump, catch at peak. | Tracking ball flight, timing approach, knee-up for protection. | 3 sets of 12 reps. | **Harder:** Throw ball slightly forward/backward to force movement. |
| 2 | **Cross Claiming Under Pressure** | 👥 | Ball, goal, server, 2 attackers ⚽ | Server delivers crosses. Attackers challenge (passive/active). | Decision (come/stay), internal "Keeper!" call, technique in traffic. | 15 reps from each side. | **Harder:** Add defenders to make the box more crowded. |
| 3 | **Punch Drill — Directional** | 👥 | Ball, goal, target cones ⚽ | Punches too high/far to catch. Punch to target zones (wide). | Fist form (flat surface), punch distance, clearing to safety. | 10 reps per side. | **Harder:** Coach uses a tennis racket to serve faster balls. |

## U17–U18 — Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Jump & Catch Plyometrics** | 🏠 | Ball, soft surface ⚽ | Self-toss high. Box/broad jump before exploding up to catch. | Explosiveness from jump, timing of reach, secure landing. | 4 sets of 8 reps. | **Harder:** Carry light medicine ball (2kg) for the initial jump. |
| 2 | **Game-Realistic Cross Circuit** | 👥 | Goal, 2 servers, attackers ⚽ | Alternate crosses from both flanks into 'chaos' in the box. | Decision speed, communication, organizing the 'mess' in front. | 20 total crosses. | **Harder:** Add extra balls for immediate "second ball" recovery. |
| 3 | **Corner Kick Management** | 👥 | Goal, full corner setup ⚽ | Deal with in/out swingers and short corners in a full-team setting. | Starting position, leadership, organizing the defensive block. | 12 corners total. | **Harder:** Add a 'screens' player to block the keeper's view. |

# 3. 1v1 & BREAKAWAY SITUATIONS

## U11–U13 — Introduction

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mirror Shuffle Drill** | 🏠 | Mirror/Space | Shuffle left/right/back and forward. Get "big" on command. | Ready position, balance while moving, explosive "set." | 3 sets of 2 minutes. | **Harder:** Use a partner to give visual cues instead of a mirror. |
| 2 | **1v1 Approach Drill** | 👥 | Ball, cones ⚽ | Attacker dribbles. Keeper practices approach/closing the angle. | Timing of advance, closing space, distance when getting set. | 8-10 reps. | **Harder:** Attacker can change speed or direction mid-dribble. |
| 3 | **1v1 Live — Stay Feet** | 👥 | Ball, goal, 1 attacker | Attacker goes 1v1. Keeper must stay on feet as long as possible. | Patience, reading the striker, explosive save only when shot is taken. | 10 reps. | **Harder:** Start with keeper at one post (side approach). |

## U14–U16 — Refinement

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Spread Save Shadow Work** | 🏠 | Soft surface | Practice the star-shape spread save technique without a ball. | Making self wide, head steady, hand position (fingers splayed). | 4 sets of 8 reps. | **Harder:** Add a low hurdle to hop over before dropping into spread. |
| 2 | **Rush & Retreat Decision** | 👥 | Ball, goal, 2 attackers ⚽ | Attacker 1 rushes; if they pass, keeper must retreat and reset for Attacker 2. | Rush timing, reading the pass, reset speed for shot 2. | 10 total reps. | **Harder:** Attacker 1 can shoot OR pass to keep keeper guessing. |
| 3 | **1v1 Competition** | 👥 | Ball, goal, multiple attackers | Live 1v1 mini-tournament. Track points for saves vs goals. | Competitive composure, handling different striker styles. | 10-15 live reps. | **Harder:** Start keeper with back to play (reaction start). |

## U17–U18 — Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Video Analysis & Viz** | 🏠 | Phone/Tablet | Review breakaway clips. Pause and call out your action, then visualize. | Analytical thinking, decision awareness, visualization discipline. | 6 clips per session. | **Harder:** Record yourself explaining your decision to "coach" others. |
| 2 | **Through-Ball Sweeping** | 👥 | Goal, servers, attacker ⚽ | Midfielder plays through-balls. Decide: sweep (intercept) or hold (1v1). | Reading ball speed, decision speed, distribution after sweep. | 12 reps. | **Harder:** Midfielder can shoot directly if keeper is too far off line. |
| 3 | **Penalty Shootout Sim** | 👥 | Full goal, 5 shooters | Full penalty simulation. Study shooter's body cues before they strike. | Reading cues, dive timing, staying big and central. | 10-15 penalties. | **Harder:** Use headphones with crowd noise to simulate pressure. |

# 4. DISTRIBUTION

## U8–U10 — Foundation

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Wall Passing** | 🏠 | Ball, wall | Stand 8–10 ft from wall. Roll ball firmly at target. Catch return. | Bowling technique, follow-through, accuracy, soft hands on return. | 3 sets of 15 rolls per hand. | **Harder:** Use a smaller ball or increase the bowling distance. |
| 2 | **Target Roll Game** | 👥 | Ball, 4 cones ⚽ | Set 4 targets at 5–15 yards. Roll ball to hit each target. | Weight of pass, stepping into the roll, scanning targets. | 3 rounds of all 4 targets. | **Harder:** Add a time limit (e.g., hit all 4 in 15 seconds). |
| 3 | **Short-Pass Triangle** | 👥 | Ball, 2 mates ⚽ | Keeper + 2 players in triangle (10yd). Receive, touch, pass. | First touch quality, head up before pass, pass accuracy (feet). | 3 sets of 3 minutes. | **Harder:** One-touch only or increase spacing to 15 yards. |

## U11–U13 — Development

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Overarm Throw Accuracy** | 🏠 | Ball, wall ⚽ | Mark targets at different heights. Throw javelin-style at marks. | Throwing form, stepping into target, arm extension, power. | 3 sets of 10 throws. | **Harder:** Increase distance or try throwing with your weak hand. |
| 2 | **Goal Kick Technique** | 👥 | Balls, goal ⚽ | 3 target zones (30, 40, 50 yd). Practice striking to zones. | Plant foot position, contact point (middle/bottom), trajectory. | 15 total kicks (5 per zone). | **Harder:** Practice goal kicks into a gentle headwind. |
| 3 | **Distribution Decision Game** | 👥 | Ball, goal, 4 mates ⚽ | Save ball, then distribute in 6s. 4 targets, 2 pressers. | Scanning before save, decision speed, accuracy under pressure. | 12 total reps. | **Harder:** Pressers start closer to the keeper (higher pressure). |

## U14–U16 — Refinement

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Driven Pass Against Wall** | 🏠 | Ball, wall ⚽ | Stand 15–25 yd. Driven goal kicks/half-volleys through 2ft gate. | Striking technique (laced), ball flight, accuracy, consistency. | 3 sets of 10 kicks. | **Harder:** Narrow the gate to 1 foot or increase distance to 30 yards. |
| 2 | **Counter-Attack Throw** | 👥 | Ball, goal, runners ⚽ | Save, then immediately throw to 3 runners at varying distances. | Decision speed, accuracy leading the runner, selecting right target. | 12 total reps. | **Harder:** Runners can change direction mid-sprint. |
| 3 | **Build from Back — Press** | 👥 | Ball, goal, defenders ⚽ | Play out from 18 under press from 2–3 opponents. Composure. | Composure, first touch direction, pass accuracy, scanning. | 10 total reps. | **Harder:** Add an extra striker to increase the press intensity. |

## U17–U18 — Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Half-Volley Distance** | 🏠 | Balls, Field ⚽ | Drop ball and strike at half-bounce to targets at 40–60 yards. | Drop timing, contact point, full follow-through, trajectory. | 4 sets of 8 kicks. | **Harder:** Aim at specific target markers (e.g., trash cans/hoops). |
| 2 | **Full-Game Simulation** | 👥 | Goal, Team ⚽ | 15m possession game. Keeper is integrated as 11th outfielder. | Pass %, decision quality, tempo control, reading the press. | 1 full 15-minute game. | **Harder:** Keeper only has 2 touches max per possession. |
| 3 | **Transition Throw — Finish** | 👥 | Goal, teammates ⚽ | Save, then throw to start 3v2 counter. Result shot in 10s. | Throw speed, catalytic passes, leading attackers into space. | 8 total reps. | **Harder:** Add a backtracking defender to the counter-attack. |

# 5. FOOTWORK & AGILITY

## U8–U10 — Foundation

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Ladder Dance** | 🏠 | Agility Ladder ⚽ | Basic patterns: icky-shuffle, lateral, two-in-each. | Foot light/fast, high turn rate, eyes up, rhythm. | 3 runs per pattern. | **Harder:** Hold a tennis ball or keep your eyes on the coach. |
| 2 | **Cone Shuffle & Set** | 👥 | 4 cones, Ball ⚽ | Shuffle to each cone, get in ready pos, catch ball. | No-cross feet shuffle, getting "Set" early, balance. | 4 sets of all 4 cones. | **Harder:** Coach varies throw speed or lobs vs driven balls. |
| 3 | **Follow the Leader** | 👥 | Space | Mirror coach's movements: forward, back, lateral, diagonal. | Reaction to movement cues, ready position, balance. | 3 rounds of 30 seconds. | **Harder:** Use a ball; keeper must hold it while mirroring. |

## U11–U13 — Development

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Box Agility Circuit** | 🏠 | 4 cones (square) ⚽ | Sprint forward → shuffle → backpedal → shuffle. | Transition speed, low center of gravity, sharp turns. | 6 reps each direction. | **Harder:** Add a ball at the last cone for a scoop save. |
| 2 | **Power Step Drill** | 👥 | Ball, cones, goal ⚽ | Explosive power step on command, then dive to save. | Explosiveness, pushing off trailing foot, covering ground. | 5 reps each side (3 sets). | **Harder:** Add a low hurdle to jump over for the power step. |
| 3 | **Crossover & Save** | 👥 | Ball, goal, cones ⚽ | Use crossover step for far shot coverage, then dive. | Crossover technique, covering ground fast, recovery set. | 8 reps each side. | **Harder:** Start seated to increase recovery/get-set challenge. |

## U14–U16 — Refinement

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **T-Test Speed Drill** | 🏠 | 4 cones ⚽ | Sprint forward, shuffle left/right, backpedal. Time it. | Agility transitions, balance, speed, foot turnover rate. | 5 runs (timed). | **Harder:** Touch each cone with your hand to increase depth. |
| 2 | **Recovery & Reposition** | 👥 | Ball, goal, 2 servers ⚽ | Dive left, recover, reposition center, faces shot right (3s). | Recovery speed, getting "Set" (feet planted), repositioning line. | 4 sets of 6 reps. | **Harder:** Servers can shoot or pass to keep keeper guessing. |
| 3 | **Angle-Change Sprint** | 👥 | Cones, goal, shooter ⚽ | Sprint to cone called, adjust angle, get set, save. | Speed of adjustment, getting "Set" before shot, depth. | 15 total reps. | **Harder:** Add 2 shooters who can pass before taking the shot. |

## U17–U18 — Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **GK Plyometric Circuit** | 🏠 | Box/Cones ⚽ | Box jump, lateral bounds, depth jumps, single-leg stick. | Explosiveness, landing mechanics, stabilizer strength. | 3 full circuits. | **Harder:** Add a medicine ball for the bounds and jumps. |
| 2 | **Chaos Footwork Drill** | 👥 | Balls, goal, cones ⚽ | commands: shuffle/drop/sprint. Whistle = save immediate shot. | Set position ready from any movement, reaction speed. | 3 rounds of 90 seconds. | **Harder:** Add visual distractions (e.g., lights or screen). |
| 3 | **Match-Simulation Move** | 👥 | Goal, full game ⚽ | Track repositioning movements during an 11v11 session. | Proactive movement, adjusting line with ball, idle time efficiency. | 1 full training match. | **Harder:** Keeper acts as sweeper for a high defensive line. |

# 6. TACTICAL AWARENESS & DECISION-MAKING

## U10 — Foundation (Simple)

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The Shadow Game** | 🏠 | Ball, open space | Place ball (shooter). Position self between ball and center goal. | Concept of "the angle," ball-goal line positioning. | 12 position checks. | **Harder:** Move the ball faster and adjust on the run. |
| 2 | **Angle String Drill** | 👥 | String, ball ⚽ | Tie string from posts to ball to show shooting "V". | Visualizing the shooting lane, finding narrowest gap. | 10 position changes. | **Harder:** Remove the string and check if the keeper stays in the lane. |
| 3 | **Coach Says: Come/Stay** | 👥 | Ball, small goal ⚽ | Coach sets scenarios (ball close/far). Keeper decides and explains. | Positional logic, risk assessment, decision speed. | 15 scenarios. | **Harder:** Add a timer (1s to decide). |

## U11–U13 — Development

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Film Study — Positioning** | 🏠 | Phone/tablet | Watch match film. Pause and mark where keeper should be. | Analytical theory, understanding the ball-goal line. | 10m session. | **Harder:** Review professional clips with high-speed movement. |
| 2 | **Position-Adjust-Save** | 👥 | Balls, goal, 3 cones ⚽ | Pass ball between 3 players. Keeper adjusts with every pass. | Constant adjustment, reading danger, getting "Set" before shot. | 15 reps. | **Harder:** One player can shoot at any time without warning. |
| 3 | **Small-Sided Game Focus** | 👥 | Small goal, 4v4/5v5 | Play in SSG with focus on positioning logic. Coach tracks moves. | Game-flow application, decision speed, reading team shape. | 1 full SSG game. | **Harder:** Increase pitch size to force more sweeper-keeper movement. |

## U14–U16 — Refinement

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Tactical Film Journal** | 🏠 | Notebook | Journal professional keeper actions for one half of a match. | Tactical eye, pro-level understanding, analytical growth. | 45m session. | **Harder:** Present findings to your coach. |
| 2 | **Sweeper Decision Drill** | 👥 | Goal, 3 players ⚽ | Midfielder plays through-balls. Real-time sweep or hold decision. | Decision speed, reading ball speed/angle, communication. | 12 total reps. | **Harder:** Midfielder can take the ball themselves for a 1v1. |
| 3 | **Set-Piece Organization** | 👥 | Goal, Team ⚽ | Organize wall/marking for corners and free kicks. | Wall placement, command clarity, defensive logic. | 8 set pieces. | **Harder:** Assign "unmarked" players to see if keeper notices. |

## U17–U18 — Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Self-Scouting Report** | 🏠 | Film, Notebook | Review own match. Grade positioning, distribution, and comms. | Self-awareness, analytical honesty, pattern recognition. | 1 full match review. | **Harder:** Compare your scouting report with a teammate's view. |
| 2 | **High-Line Defense Game** | 👥 | Full Pitch ⚽ | Play as sweeper-keeper with a high defensive line. | Proactive reading, sweeping technique, risk management. | 1 full match. | **Harder:** Play with a 1-man disadvantage on the back line. |
| 3 | **Decision Pressure Cooker** | 👥 | Goal, Players ⚽ | Rapid-fire tactical scenarios. 2s to call and execute. | Decision speed, accuracy under pressure, verbal composure. | 20 scenarios. | **Harder:** Add distracting crowd noise or verbal "heckling". |

# 7. COMMUNICATION & LEADERSHIP

## U10–U13 — Introduction

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Mirror Commands** | 🏠 | Mirror | Call out commands: "KEEPER!", "AWAY!", "DROP!". | Volume, clarity, authoritative body language. | 3 sets of all commands. | **Harder:** Record yourself and play back to check clarity/fear factor. |
| 2 | **Blind Defending** | 👥 | Defenders, Attacker ⚽ | Direct a blindfolded defender using verbal cues ONLY. | Command specificity, timing, volume, direction accuracy. | 6 reps. | **Harder:** Use 2 defenders who both need directing simultaneously. |
| 3 | **Post-Play Call-Out** | 👥 | Game setting | Give one clear instruction after every dead ball stoppage. | Specificity, habit building, leadership presence. | 10+ call-outs per session. | **Harder:** Commands must include a tactical fix for the next play. |

## U14–U18 — Development & Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Captain's Monologue** | 🏠 | Voice recorder | Record 60s halftime talk for specific match scenarios. | Leadership voice, tactical content, confidence, composure. | 3 scenarios per week. | **Harder:** Do it in front of a mirror or a live audience. |
| 2 | **Defensive Shape Drill** | 👥 | Back 4 defenders | Verbally organize the back 4 as attackers pass the ball. | Specificity (names), early calls, adjusting to ball. | 10 minutes. | **Harder:** Attackers can "cheat" (start offside) — keeper must catch it. |
| 3 | **Post-Match Debrief** | 👥 | Whiteboard | Lead a 3m defensive debrief after a training match. | Leadership initiative, constructive feedback, tactical awareness. | 1 debrief session. | **Harder:** Must list at least 3 positives for every 1 negative fix. |

# 8. MENTAL & PSYCHOLOGICAL

## U10–U13 — Foundation

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The Reset Routine** | 🏠 | No equipment | Create and practice a 3-breath focus reset after conceding. | Routine consistency, composure, psychological anchoring. | 5 reps x 5 days. | **Harder:** Practice the routine while physically fatigued. |
| 2 | **Mistake Challenge** | 👥 | Training setting | Say ONE positive check after every physical mistake in a drill. | Positive self-talk, reframing, resilience building. | All drill mishaps. | **Harder:** Must also say one technical fix for the mistake. |
| 3 | **Focus Timer** | 👥 | Game setting | Coach secretly rates focus in 3 random 5m windows. | Concentration endurance, body language, proactive comms. | 3 windows per game. | **Harder:** Coach tells everyone EXCEPT the keeper to distract them. |

## U14–U18 — Development & Mastery

| # | Drill | Type | Setup | How It Works | Focus Points | Volume | Variation |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Visualization Routine** | 🏠 | Quiet space | Visualize 5 match scenarios (save, cross, 1v1, dist, mistake). | Detail of visualization, routine discipline, emotional control. | 10m x 3 per week. | **Harder:** "Feel" the temperature, wind, and grass in the viz. |
| 2 | **Pressure Simulation** | 👥 | Full goal, speakers ⚽ | Perform technical drill with crowd noise and verbal distractions. | Focus under noise, composure, technique consistency. | 15 minutes. | **Harder:** Teammates actively try to make the keeper laugh/break focus. |
| 3 | **Journaling & Reflection** | 🏠 | Notebook | Write (1) Well (2) Different (3) Focus after every session. | Self-awareness, pattern recognition, growth mindset. | Every training/match. | **Harder:** Present your journal entries during 1-on-1 coach meetings. |
